Details & Eligibility
01
Open Applications
We accept applications year-round. No prior modelling experience required — only the right energy, work ethic, and look. All genders welcome.
02
Duration
The full program runs 12 weeks with flexible scheduling. Individual track sessions are available for those already active in the industry.
03
Safety First
NDA will never ask for payment to join our roster or program. All mentorship fees are fixed and disclosed upfront. Read our full safety guidelines before applying.
